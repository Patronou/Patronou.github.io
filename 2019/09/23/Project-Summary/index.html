Project Summary

2019-09-23

What is my project?

Retail Credit Risk Model Based on Data Science

What are my research target and research method?

My research target is to build a credit scoring model for loss given default. My research method includes the use of exploratory data analysis, data cleansing, machine learning, and feature engineering.

What was the biggest challenge that I encountered? How did I overcome it?

The biggest challenge I encountered is unfamiliar functions and data types. I learned extra knowledge of coding to overcame it.

What are my research result(s) and conclusion(s)?

My research result is the credit scoring model based on the database provided by Lending Club.
